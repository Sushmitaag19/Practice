Gold Price Fluctuation Analysis 
From 1978 - 2026

#Tool used:
 - Power BI

#Description
- This analysis simply focuses on the rise and fall on the price of gold form year 1978 to 2026 (January). The analysis for now does not include the factors causing the changes, rather it just studies the increase and decrease on the prices based on various currencies and visualizes the changes with the use of various charts. 

#KPI 
- Maximum Price
- Minimum Price
- Average Price

#Data Collection
- For this analysis, The data was collected from: https://www.gold.org/goldhub/data
