# Airbnb Data Analytics for year 2026

## objectives
To analyze the KPI that affects overall ratings, pricing and bookings of various Airbnb in US

## Tools used
Tableau Public

## KPI
- No. of bedrooms
- Location
- Price based on bedrooms
